Controls:

  • W/S: Forward/Backward
  • A/D: Turn Left/Right
  • Q/E: Roll Left/Right
  • Arrow Keys: Strafe
  • Space: Fire Weapons
  • T: Cycle Next Target
  • Y: Cycle Previous Target
  • R: Clear Target
  • C/Tab: Cycle Weapon
  • Shift: Afterburner
  • F1-F3: Power to Weapons/Engines/Shields
  • F4: Balanced Power
  • Z/X: Shield Forward/Rear
  • V: Balance Shields
  • M: View Mission Objectives
